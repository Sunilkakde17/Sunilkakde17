## About Me

I'm a Computer Science (Data Science) student at Shreeyash College of Engineering & Technology, currently doing a data science internship at ATH Innovations. Outside of coursework, I build and ship real products — most of what's here comes from actual projects, not tutorials.

Right now I split my time between two things:

- **RGTvertex** — an AI-workforce platform I'm building with React, Next.js, Tailwind, Framer Motion, and Supabase. Been working through everything from the core UI to a full careers/jobs system (listings, application flow, database schema).
- A **B2B lead-generation product** for a small startup, targeting Indian IT/software services firms — I handle a lot of the data verification and outreach tooling side of it.

Before this, my main project was **Graphura** (formerly ScamShield) — a fake job/internship scam detector with a FastAPI backend, a Supabase/PostgreSQL database, and a Claude-powered chatbot for explaining why a listing looks fishy. It's the project that taught me most of what I know about full-stack + AI integration, and it was the center of my internship review.

I like building things end-to-end — backend, frontend, and the product thinking in between — rather than just one layer of the stack.
